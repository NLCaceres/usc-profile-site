Assignment 8: The Final Project - The Latest Car Site for All Your Next Car Info Needs! Car Lords

For the last assignment from ITP 301 at USC, I decided I would try and make something in the style of Car Gurus, incorporating two jQuery plugins to implement a slider and a modal popover alongside W3CSS. By making requests to the Edmunds API, I'd swap out components to display articles and nearby cars that relate to the user's search in place of the base URL's display. The trick was doing so without a front-end framework and simply in jQuery and vanilla JavaScript.
